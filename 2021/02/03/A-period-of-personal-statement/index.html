A period of personal statement

Qustions:

Tell us about who you are. How would your family, friends, and/or members of your community describe you? If possible, please include something about yourself that you are most proud of and why.

Statement:

Presently, I am studying in Jin Ling High School international department. I am a good communicator and listener; I can express my opinion clearly when communicating with others. At the same time, I am also able to listen to and understand othersâ€™ points in order to perfect my own stance. As a consequence, I can think about one question from different angles, listening helps develop my thinking ability a lot.

People around me always describe me as an organized person with creative ideas. The reason for this is that I can always arrange things well in quite a short time as well as come up with wonderful design during discussions. In addition, they think that I am the kind of person who always very calm when dealing with problems.

Among all the subjects, mathematics stands out as my best subject. I am pretty good at mathematics, and I am especially good with numbers. I am excellent at remembering a string of numbers like phone numbers, the ID card and account numbers and so on. I rarely make any mistakes in memorizing numbers. I am most proud of being the kind of person who likes digging into something and solving the problem myself.

Once, there seemed to be something wrong with my computer, some software sometimes closed due to a programming running error. However, it was evident that there was nothing wrong with the software as I had downloaded some of the software again from their official website. The software continued having the same problem as before. I thought that there must be something strange with the system, but not the software themselves. I tried to find the answer to the question via the internet. Unfortunately, after I tried all kinds of solutions to solve the problem, the problem was still there. I decided to check the errors of the modules which are related to the software. Hard work pays off, it was interesting that the only small error was an unusual setting which I discovered by testing a small programme. I felt so thoroughly proud of myself for solving the tough problem.
