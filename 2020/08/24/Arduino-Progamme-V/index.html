Arduino Progamme V

A whole new programme was started in this class which is the automatic handwriting machine.

Firstly, we built up several parts of basic structure.

We combined them together and then tight the up them with nails like the picture.

We found out that when we tried to put the nail into some hole with serrate it could be a quite tough task for us. The nail is so tight that we nearly destroy the nail with too much force in order to tight pieces together.

Then, we installed the servo on the board to make the machine’s pen able to move in different directions.

[](https://imgchr.com/i/dsOvxx)

Finally, we combined all these units together to make a basic model of our work.

](https://imgchr.com/i/dsXSsK)

It is so scared for me that I broke one of a small combination as its size doesn’t fit the others well. When it happened, I thought that how bad the quality of our material is. But, later, I thought that maybe I am so impatient and I used too large force.

[](https://imgchr.com/i/dsXpqO)

We tested for the servo after we complete the model, just like the picture above.

I really learnt a lot from this class
