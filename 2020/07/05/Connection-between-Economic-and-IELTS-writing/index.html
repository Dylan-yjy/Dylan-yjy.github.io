Connection between Economic and IELTS writing

​ Through the learning of the IELTS class, it is interested to find out that there is a close connection between Economic problem and the IELTS writing.

​ Many method of analysis economic problem can be used properly and suitably in writing. Such as, when the question ask you about whether the building of a new bridge in a rural area is benefit or not. And it can be explained as that the building of the bridge can reduce the cost of transportation which can reduce the price of the goods and lower the selling price of the goods which is produced by the village like crops. The lower price makes the goods form that area has more competitiveness than the others, so the demands for the goods increase which helps people gain more revenue from their goods. And it helps increase their income and they are able to improve their living standards.

​ The building of bridge makes the transport between cities and the rural area more convince it enable to push the development of the travelling industry. The culture was also able to be developed as the development of the travelling industry.

​ From the analysis above, we can see the methods of explaining economic problems. I was thought about that the examiner may be just want the exam takers to develop an ability of thinking about our society and try to think about the problem we are facing and trying to solve. Just like the economic problems, to answer these questions we need a clear logic between each explanation you want to show to the examiner.

It helps me a lot to write some of the opinions by using some economic knowledge and I think that we are necessary to try to put our knowledge in series.
