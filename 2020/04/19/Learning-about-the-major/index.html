Learning about the major

I learnt about the Electric and Electronic Engineering major from the official website of the university this week. I got an integrated understanding of the major from the subject I should learn if I choose the major to the project we may take part in in the 3 year study.

We will learn about different subjects such as analogue and digital electronics, communication and control, alongside mathematics and computing modules in year 1 and year 2. Of course, we are able to choose extra subjects which we interested in. In year 3, we will choose the topic we like in a series of widen and boarden topics of electrical and electronic engineering.

The reason why I am interested in this major is that the Electric and Electronic Engineering is a major at the forefront of the challenge to connect our world. I can learn more about cutting-edge knowledge of the world and I am able to use the knowledge to innovate and try to improve some devices to benefit the society.

We will be fully equipped to demonstrate all your knowledge, skills and innovation in a large and ambitious individual project in our final year. I think that it would be a great chance to show myself and get precious experience of cooperation and innovation.

I really learnt a lot from the website and I will learn about more majors in the future from the website.
